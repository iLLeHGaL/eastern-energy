Licensed Electrician

Full-time Experience: 3+ years
Apply Now

We're looking for experienced, licensed electricians to join our growing team. You'll work on a variety of residential and commercial projects, from new construction to service calls.

Requirements:
  • Valid state electrical license
  • 3+ years of experience in residential and/or commercial electrical work
  • Strong troubleshooting and problem-solving skills
  • Valid driver's license and reliable transportation
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team

Electrical Apprentice

Full-time Entry Level
Apply Now

Start your career in the electrical trade with our apprenticeship program. Learn from experienced journeymen while earning competitive wages and working toward your license.

Requirements:
  • High school diploma or GED
  • Strong interest in learning the electrical trade
  • Basic math and reading comprehension skills
  • Valid driver's license
  • Willingness to attend trade school classes

Service Technician

Full-time Experience: 2+ years
Apply Now

Join our service team to handle residential and light commercial service calls. This role requires excellent customer service skills and the ability to diagnose and repair electrical issues efficiently.

Requirements:
  • Valid state electrical license (Journeyman or higher)
  • 2+ years of service/repair experience
  • Excellent customer communication skills
  • Strong diagnostic and troubleshooting abilities
  • Clean driving record
